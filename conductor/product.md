# Product Guide: Industrious Flight School

## Overview

Industrious Flight School is an internal employee onboarding and training management platform designed for Industrious, a premium flexible workspace company. The "Flight School" name embodies the journey new team members take—from their first day to becoming fully onboarded, confident contributors ready to deliver exceptional member experiences.

## Target Users

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Operations/HR Admins** | Manage the overall onboarding program, configure training content, track cohort progress, and ensure consistency across locations |
| **Managers** | Oversee and support their direct reports through onboarding, complete manager tasks, provide feedback, and monitor progress |
| **New Hires** | Navigate their personalized onboarding journey, complete training modules, track their progress, and connect with their team |

## Product Goals

1. **Streamline & Standardize** — Deliver a consistent onboarding experience across all Industrious locations, ensuring every new hire receives the same foundational training and cultural immersion.

2. **Track Progress & Accountability** — Provide visibility into new hire progress, training completion rates, and timeline adherence for both individuals and cohorts.

3. **Empower Manager Support** — Give managers the tools to actively support their new hires through structured task lists, feedback mechanisms, and recognition features.

## Core Features

### Role-Based Dashboards
Tailored experiences for each user type:
- **Admin Console** — Program-wide oversight with views for workflow management, cohort tracking, agenda planning, communications, and engagement analytics
- **Manager Dashboard** — Team-focused view to monitor direct reports, complete onboarding tasks, and provide support
- **New Hire Dashboard** — Personal journey view with training modules, progress tracking, and upcoming milestones

### Training Module System
Flexible content delivery supporting multiple formats:
- Workbooks (self-paced reading and reflection)
- Video content
- Live calls and virtual sessions
- Shadowing and peer learning
- Manager-led training
- Performance tasks

### OKR Management
Goal-setting framework connecting new hires to business objectives from day one, with role-specific objectives and measurable key results.

## Key Differentiator

**Manager Onboarding Tracker** — A pre-configured task system with timeline-relative due dates (e.g., "7 days before start," "Day 1," "Week 2") that guides managers through every step of preparing for and supporting a new hire. This ensures nothing falls through the cracks and creates accountability for manager involvement in onboarding success.

## Brand & Metaphor

The "Flight School" concept positions onboarding as a transformative journey:
- New hires are learning to "fly" — gaining the skills, knowledge, and confidence to operate independently
- Training modules are "flight lessons" building toward readiness
- Completion represents being "cleared for takeoff" as a full team member
